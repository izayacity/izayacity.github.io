About Me

LinkedIn Twitter Github Email Resume


I'm currently a graduate student studying Games and Playable Media at University of California Santa Cruz. I received a Bachelor Degree in Network Engineering of Computer Science in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications from 2011 to 2015. I interned as a web developer in Beijing and Tokyo before coming to San Francisco Bay Area. I'm a motivated, proactive and persevering programmer who is keen on the latest tools. Due to the previous working responsibilities, I'm equipped with variety of programming skills such as PHP, Javascript, python, Golang, C# and C++, as well as development tools such as Bitbucket, Jira, AWS and GAE. During UCSC, I successfully got to develop with variety of game engines such as SFML, Unity and Unreal, and be equipped with UI, audio and gameplay design skills. Although being as a generalist undermines my competitiveness as a professional, I gained a lot of fun from these experiences and held a open mind towards any challenges.

My Email address is izayacity@gmail.com

My Skills

C++ is the language I used the longest so far. I have developed on several C++ frameworks or game engines like QT, Snort, SFML, Unreal Engine 4, etc.

I began to write my first PHP program in my sophomore year and had been developed on LAMP, WAMP and MAMP. During my internship in Tokyo, I developed a management system independently using Slim framework.

Go is my current favorite language in addition to Swift because of its beautiful and concise grammar, and its efficiency. I used it to make data visualization by GQL and memcache on GAE.

Python is my first script language that I used to develop a DNS relay parser in college. During my internship in Beijing, I was developing under Django framework. I also use Python to process data analysis.

Because of network engineering major and cyber-security research during college, I had been using Ubuntu from 11.04 to 14.10, as well as CentOS. Currently I'm only using Mac OSX.

Javascript is also my favourite language as it is multi-platform and could be used in functional paradigm. I had experience with jQuery, Ionic (based on AngularJS), Bootstrap, ThreeJS, ChartJS, MetroJS, etc.

During UCSC, I contributed developing Hive in a pair, a 3D third-person VR hacking game by Unity3d on Oculus in a pair. Then I contributed developing Tornado Tower, A 3D third-person VR interactive platformer game by Unreal Engine 4 on Oculus.

I was researching network engineering and cyber-security during college and my internship in Beijing. Later I worked on AWS and GAE and contributed to developing lesson-time.com and deployed tornado-tower.com on AWS.

I'm equipped with knowledge and experience of software engineering, UML documentation, Agile development and management. I also developed management systems for both of my internship companies in Beijing and Tokyo.
