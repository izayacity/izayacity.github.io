About Me

I'm currently a graduate student studying Games and Playable Media at University of California Santa Cruz. I received a Bachelor Degree in Network Engineering of Computer Science in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications from 2011 to 2015. I interned as a web developer in Beijing and Tokyo before coming to San Francisco Bay Area. I'm a motivated and persevering programmer who is keen on the most optimized tech solution. Due to the previous working responsibilities, I worked with a variety of programming skills such as PHP, Javascript, python, Golang, C# and C++, as well as development tools such as Bitbucket, Jira, AWS and GAE. During UCSC, I successfully got to develop with a variety of game engines or graphic libraries such as Unity, Unreal, SFML and OpenGL and be equipped with some UI, audio and gameplay design skills. From the moment of setting up a professional website, visualizing high QPS data into charts, rendering an illusionary particle system, designing and implementing an algorithm, scripting a gameplay feature to designing organized and light-weight APIs, I gained a lot of fun from these experiences of making and finding art of programming, with an open mind towards any challenges.
